Frequently Asked Questions

Is ExpenseIQ Pro free to use?
Yes! ExpenseIQ Pro is completely free to use. Create an account and start tracking your expenses immediately.
Is my financial data secure?
Absolutely. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt, all connections use HTTPS, and data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas.
Which currencies are supported?
ExpenseIQ Pro supports USD ($), EUR (€), GBP (£), and INR (₹). You can change your preferred currency in Settings.
Can I export my data?
Yes! You can export your expense data as CSV from the Expenses page, and generate monthly reports from the Reports page.
