Mint by Intuit
Mint is a popular personal finance and budgeting application that helps users track spending, create budgets, and monitor credit scores. It connects to bank accounts for automatic transaction tracking and categorization.

Budgeting
YNAB (You Need A Budget)
YNAB is a budgeting tool based on the envelope budgeting method. It helps users give every dollar a job, break the paycheck-to-paycheck cycle, and build long-term savings habits through proven budgeting principles.
www.ynab.com

NerdWallet provides objective financial advice and tools to help consumers make smart financial decisions. It covers credit cards, banking, investing, mortgages, insurance, and personal loans with unbiased reviews and comparisons.
